Ferrari 488 GTB

The Ferrari 488 GTB is a mid-engine sports car known for its powerful twin-turbocharged V8 engine, advanced aerodynamics, and engaging driving experience. It features a 3.9-liter V8 producing 661 horsepower, and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in around 3 seconds. The "488" in its name refers to the engine's unitary displacement, while "GTB" stands for Gran Turismo Berlinetta
